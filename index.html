Moab Hikes Project

This project identifies a curated list of hiking trails around Moab, Utah, based on proximity, scenic value, and logistical viability for a multi-day outdoor experience.

Criteria Used

  • Within 30 miles of Moab
  • Under 8 miles round-trip
  • Offers unique features: arches, water, solitude, or petroglyphs
  • Hikeable in March weather conditions
